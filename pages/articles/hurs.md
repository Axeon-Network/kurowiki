The **Horicraft Underground Rail System** (HURS) is a underground railway network mainly located in [Fucking](Fucking,_NovaLand), [NovaLand](NovaLand), also connecting to the NovaLandese cities of [Ture](Ture,_NovaLand) and [Jell](Jell,_NovaLand).

In [Horicraft](Horicraft), most of the network was built in January 2025 in the spawn area, later inherited by [MidoriMC](MidoriMC) when it started hosting Horicraft's world. 8 HURS stations have been built by March 2025, of which 5 are located the capital city of Fucking (including the largest one), 2 are located in Jell, and 1 in Ture.

While most of the railway network is underground, some sections of HURS railway may also run on ground level, primarily in Jell.

# Horicraft Water Transportation System
The **Horicraft Water Transportation System** (HWTS) is a network of waterways in and around Fucking, NovaLand. It is aimed to improve passage through Fucking while reducing travel times without having to leave boat. It is an alternative to HURS.
