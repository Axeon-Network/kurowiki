**Nishi** is a fictional character created by [Kit](Kit64). It is Kit's fursona since the introduction of the Kit64 branding on 15 November 2025.

# Physical appearance
Nishi's body is mainly light gray-furred, such as their body, tail tip and head, but their legs and tail (excluding the tip) are gray. Additionally, their snout, paws are feet are white, and their ears and paw beans are dark gray.
