**Kit64** (alternatively spelled **KitSixtyFour**), commonly known as **Kit**[^1] (born 19 June 2010) is a Honduran YouTuber, developer, gamer and self-proclaimed "[professional tech breaker](Kit_Moments)". They are a member of the [Axeon Network](Axeon_Network), primarally known for developing [KuroWiki](KuroWiki) alongside [KayAurora](KayAurora).

They are the creator of several projects such as [StuffyXP](StuffyXP), [HoriWebsite](HoriWebsite), [Horicraft](Horicraft), [HoriServer](HoriServer) (and its [Discord counterpart](HoriServer_(2))), [amongst several others](List_of_Kit64's_projects), and are the founder of [AstroNT](AstroNT), [Comet Adventure](Comet_Adventure) and KuroWiki.

# Personal life
Kit was born on 19 June 2010 in Juticalpa, Honduras, and stayed there for a couple of months due to preterm-related medical issues until they moved to Tegucigalpa a couple of months later. Kit is also often prone to migraines due to solar contact (even in the slightest) and sometimes wears sunglasses.

Kit is known to have Atenttion Defecit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) and thus struggles to work on projects frequently, and is known to have misophonia and <small>(somewhat)</small> constantly has to wear ear plugs or headphones. 

Kit has been a member of the [furry fandom](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Furry_fandom) since August 2025. [Their fursona](Nishi) was revealed in November 2025.

Kit is also known to be aroace[^2] and agender since May 2025. Kit goes by *they/them* pronouns and uses the Central American time zone (UTC-6).
