**KayAurora** (formerly known as **Clyron**, **Lynxmic** and **solarixtech**) (born 17 August[^1]), also known as just **Kay** or **Aurie**, is an European[^1] content creator and developer, (occasional) gamer and graphics designer.

The founder and one of the administrators of [Axeon Network](Axeon_Network) (including the Discord Server, [KuroWiki](KuroWiki) and [MidoriMC](MidoriMC)), they're also the creator of several other projects such as the [Lynxmic](Lynxmic) YouTube channel and several Discord Bots (primarily [Venus](Venus) and [Sapphire](Sapphire)), [among others](List_of_KayAurora's_projects). Their personal website is [KayWebsite](KayWebsite).
